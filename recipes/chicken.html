Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (1 per person)
  • Honey
  • Seasame seeds
  • Soy sauce
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Garlic/Garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Slice breasts in half
  2. Coat them in flour and Garlic powder if not using raw garlic
  3. Cook chicken in pan until fully closed off on both sides
  4. Add honey, soy sauce, butter and an extra tea spoon of garlic powder/crushed half clove of garlic to the pan and mix
  5. Cook until sauce reduces then cover with seeds and serve
