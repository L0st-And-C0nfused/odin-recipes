Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Pasta sauce
  • Oregano
  • Parmesean cheese
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Place a pasta sheet in an oiled tray
  2. Cover sheet in pasta sauce and sprinkle with oregano and cheese
  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until at desired height
  4. Cook in oven for 30 Minuites at 3000 degrees kelvin
  5. Enjoy your tray full of molten lava!
